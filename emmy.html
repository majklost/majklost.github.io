Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18.12.2024

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our Google Chrome extension Emmy Extension, further referred as "The Extension".

1. Data Collection and Use

Our Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. The Extension is designed to operate without requiring any personal information from users.

2. Access to Google Drive

The Extension requires access to your Google Drive for the sole purpose of:

  • Creating and managing files specifically generated by the Extension.
  • Reading, modifying, or deleting files created by the Extension itself.

The Extension does not access, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive. Access is granted via the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file scope and complies with Google's API Services User Data Policy.

3. User Consent and Revocation

By using the Extension, you consent to its use of Google Drive access as described.

You can revoke the Extension’s permissions at any time via your Google Account settings at: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Extension is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly access or process information from children.

5. Compliance with GDPR

Our operations are based in Europe, specifically in the Czech Republic. Although the Extension does not collect or process personal data, we respect the following user rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: You may request information about how the Extension interacts with your Google Drive.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can revoke permissions granted to the Extension at any time via your Google account settings.

For additional information or concerns, please contact us at tabletordinacniv2@gmail.com .

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect updates to the Extension or changes in applicable laws. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the Extension, please contact us at: tabletordinacniv2@gmail.com .
