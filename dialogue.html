Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06.05.2025

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our Google Chrome extension, Emmy Dialogue AI, referred to as “the Extension”.

1. Data Collection and Use

The Extension does not collect, store, or retain any personal or sensitive user data. It is designed to ensure your privacy by not associating any data with individual users.

2. Audio Processing and Use of Microsoft Azure

The Extension allows users to record their voice, which is then temporarily sent to a secure server for the sole purpose of converting speech to text using Microsoft Azure’s Speech-to-Text API.

  • The server does not store any recordings or transcripts.
  • Audio data is processed transiently and securely, then discarded immediately after receiving a response from Azure.
  • No personal identifiers or metadata are stored or linked to recordings.

3. User Consent

By using the Extension, you consent to the temporary processing of your audio recordings for speech-to-text conversion via Microsoft Azure.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Extension is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect or process any information from children.

5. Compliance with GDPR

Our operations are based in Europe, specifically in the Czech Republic. While no personal data is collected or stored, we respect the principles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right to Access: You may contact us to learn how the Extension functions in relation to audio data processing.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may stop using the Extension at any time. As the Extension does not store data, no further action is required for data deletion.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or in applicable regulations. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
